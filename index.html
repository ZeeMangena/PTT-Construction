Plastic Trash Treasure.

23000 of the total 32658 tonnes of the waste dumped in Sudan annually is non-biodegradable plastic, and only 1% is recycled. The rest is dumped in landfills and water bodies.

We do our part to change that by recycling the plastic into building materials.
